k_temporal_padding R Documentation

Pads the middle dimension of a 3D tensor.

Description

Pads the middle dimension of a 3D tensor.

Usage

k_temporal_padding(x, padding = c(1, 1))

Arguments

x

Tensor or variable.
padding

List of 2 integers, how many zeros to add at the start and end of dim 1.

Value

A padded 3D tensor.
