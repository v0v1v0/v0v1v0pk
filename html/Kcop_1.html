Kcop-package R Documentation

Kcop: Smooth Test for Equality of Copulas and Clustering Multivariate

Description

Implements approaches of non-parametric smooth test to compare simultaneously K(K>1) copulas and non-parametric clustering of multivariate populations with arbitrary sizes. See Yves I. Ngounou Bakam and Denys Pommeret (2022) <arXiv:2112.05623> and Yves I. Ngounou Bakam and Denys Pommeret (2022) <arXiv:2211.06338>.
